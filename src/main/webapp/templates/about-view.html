What is it?

The map shows a variety of freely available Open Government Data from the UK Highways Agency.

  • A-roads and motorways down to the roundabout and slip road level. Full details
  • Live traffic information, speed, vehicle flow per hour, flow breakdown by vehicle length - categories 1-4, road occupancy etc..
  • Variable message signs (VMS) and matrix signals with the live settings and street view integration. Signs supported. VMS pictograms supported
  • Unplanned events, e.g. accidents, congestion etc.

Geeky details

Developed using Backbone, underscore, jQuery, requirejs, jasmine, blanket, moment, Google maps V3. Backend processing with Ruby using gems including nokogiri, rmagick, polylines, haversine and proj4rb.
