These are the signs I drew for the map. They are all based on a 13x11 grid like the original and best MS1 matrix signals for an old-school retro feel.

However MS1 do not have red mandatory speed red rings. I did this for two reasons, 1) the network model supplied by NTIS does not differentiate between MS1, MS3, MS4 and AMI matrix signals 2) to simplify the design.
